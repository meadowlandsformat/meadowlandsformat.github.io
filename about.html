YCS Meadowlands

YCS Meadowlands New Jersey took place on May 12, 2013.
This event is after the release of Cosmo Blazer and uses the March 2013 Banlist.
Many of the previous decks - Rabbit, Wind-Up, and Inzektor, were hit, but are still playable here.
New cards from Abyss Rising and Cosmo Blazer - Mermail and Fire Fist - were added to the metagame at this time.
